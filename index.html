About Averdon Partners

Search for asset-heavy, safety-critical operations

Averdon Partners is a specialist executive & technical search firm focused on aviation, aerospace and complex engineering. We sit on the spine between operations and the boardroom – starting with critical technical roles and growing into retained leadership and executive mandates as relationships deepen.

Every hire is treated as a commercial, risk and governance decision. We map the market, test for depth, and only introduce candidates we would trust with a fleet, an asset base or a regulatory relationship.

  • COOs & CEOs. Stabilise operations, de-risk board and regulator conversations, and build a leadership bench that can actually carry growth.
  • Heads of Maintenance. Improve coverage, reduce AOG exposure and bring discipline to line and base maintenance without burning out your teams.
  • Heads of Engineering. Strengthen design, certification and programme delivery capability while staying inside safety, quality and cost constraints.

Approach

Low-volume, research-led, founder-driven

Our process is built for regulated, asset-heavy environments where the cost of a bad hire is measured in more than recruitment fees. We work the way you expect your own teams to operate as a COO, CEO, Head of Maintenance or Head of Engineering: disciplined, documented and with clear, low-noise communication throughout.

01. Define
Brief & mandate definition

We start with your operating reality: fleet, assets, regulatory exposure, stakeholder expectations and internal politics. The brief is shaped around risk and outcomes, not just a job description.

02. Map
Research & market mapping

We build a structured view of the market: where the right people sit, how they are measured, and what would move them. Target lists are agreed before outreach begins.

03. Assess
Shortlist & structured interviews

Candidates are assessed against technical depth, leadership behaviour and fit with your operating environment. You see a small, defensible shortlist – not a stack of CVs.

04. Close
Offer, onboarding & follow-up

We support offer design, resignation management and onboarding. After placement, we stay close to both sides to ensure the hire lands as intended.

Founder

Technical literacy, legal awareness, long-term view

Averdon Partners is founder-led. The firm was built by an aerospace engineering graduate with hands-on experience in aeronautical maintenance and a route into aviation and asset-finance law.

Aerospace engineering background · Lufthansa Technik training · Route into PGDL & SQE (aviation / asset-finance focus)

This mix of hangar-floor exposure and legal training shapes how we run search. We read technical documentation, understand maintenance and operational constraints, and think in terms of contracts, liability and governance.

For clients, that means a search partner who can sit beside you as COO, CEO, Head of Maintenance or Head of Engineering and bridge conversations between engineering, operations, finance and legal. We pay attention to approvals, safety cases, SLAs and regulatory relationships – not just CV headlines.

For candidates, it means a process run by someone who understands the reality of technical and operational roles, the career paths behind them, and the way those paths intersect with evolving regulation and finance.

The focus is deliberately narrow: a small number of mandates at any one time, run directly by the founder, so that every search stands up to scrutiny from boards, regulators and owners.
