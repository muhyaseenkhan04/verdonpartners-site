Privacy Policy

This page outlines how Averdon Partners Limited collects, uses and protects personal data in the course of providing executive and technical search services.

1. Who we are

Averdon Partners Limited is a specialist executive and technical search firm focused on aviation, aerospace and complex engineering. We act as a data controller in respect of the personal data we process about clients and candidates.

2. What data we collect

We may collect and process:

  • Contact details (name, email address, telephone number, role, employer)
  • Professional information (CV, career history, education, qualifications)
  • Information provided in messages, briefs and interview notes
  • Basic technical data from website usage (such as IP address and browser type)

3. How we use personal data

We use personal data in order to:

  • Provide executive and technical search services to our clients
  • Identify and approach potential candidates for specific mandates
  • Maintain business development and relationship management records
  • Comply with our legal, regulatory and contractual obligations

4. Legal bases

We generally process personal data on the basis of legitimate interests in operating a specialist search business, and in some cases on the basis of consent or to perform a contract.

5. Sharing data

We may share personal data with clients in connection with specific mandates, and with trusted third parties who provide IT, hosting or professional services to us. We do not sell personal data.

6. Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes set out above and to meet legal and regulatory requirements, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct or delete your personal data, to object to or restrict processing, and to data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Contact

For privacy-related queries, please email info@averdonpartners.com.
