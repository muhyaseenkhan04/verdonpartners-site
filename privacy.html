Privacy Policy

This page explains how Averdon Partners Limited collects, uses and protects personal data in the course of providing executive and technical search services.

1. Who we are

Averdon Partners Limited is a specialist executive and technical search firm focused on aviation, aerospace and complex engineering. We act as a data controller in respect of the personal data we process about clients and candidates.

2. What data we collect

We may collect and process:

  • Contact details (name, email address, telephone number, role, employer)
  • Professional information (CV, career history, education, qualifications)
  • Information provided in messages, briefs and interview notes
  • Basic technical data from website usage (such as IP address and browser type)

3. How we use your data

We use personal data to:

  • Deliver executive and technical search services to clients
  • Assess candidate fit for specific mandates
  • Communicate about roles, searches and market insights
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Legal basis for processing

We rely on a combination of legitimate interests, performance of a contract and, where appropriate, consent to process data. Our legitimate interests include providing search services, maintaining client and candidate relationships and running our business responsibly.

5. Sharing your data

We may share personal data with:

  • Client organisations in connection with specific search mandates
  • Service providers who support our operations (for example, email and IT providers)
  • Professional advisers where necessary (for example, legal or compliance advice)

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

6. Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide services, manage relationships and comply with legal or regulatory requirements. We review data periodically and delete or anonymise information that is no longer needed.

7. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data in certain circumstances
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Request transfer of your data to another organisation

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy or how we handle personal data, please contact:

Email: info@averdonpartners.com

9. Updates to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with the date of the most recent revision.
